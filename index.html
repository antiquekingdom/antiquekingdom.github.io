About Our Kingdom

ANTIQUE KINGDOM is an elite Mobile Legends squad formed by passionate gamers who share a common goal - to dominate the battlefield with strategy, skill, and teamwork.

Founded in 2020, our squad has grown from a casual gaming group to a competitive force in the Mobile Legends community. We specialize in coordinated team fights, objective control, and innovative strategies that keep our opponents guessing.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds but share a common passion for Mobile Legends and a commitment to excellence. We regularly participate in tournaments, scrims, and ranked matches to continuously improve our gameplay.
